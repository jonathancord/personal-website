About Me

I enjoy working directly with technology—configuring systems, troubleshooting issues, and understanding how different components connect and function together.

Through my coursework, I have gained experience with Linux administration, networking fundamentals, virtual machines, scripting, and containerization. I am comfortable researching solutions when needed, but I also like experimenting in controlled environments to fully understand how systems operate and improve my technical skills.
